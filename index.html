ABOUT MARIO

DJ • Producer • Performer

Born to Rock
Every Dancefloor

Mario McQuay is a veteran DJ with over 25 years of experience performing at events across the country. He attributes his success to a natural talent for reading the crowd and a deep understanding of diverse musical styles, ranging from disco, R&B, and funk to house music.

Mario’s passion for music began at age five, influenced by artists like Parliament, Donna Summer, and Curtis Mayfield. His career has grown from childhood experimentation to professional sets for weddings, fashion shows, and boat parties. He has also hosted podcasts and performed on live radio, including WGCI Radio 107.5FM in Chicago and 88.3 FM (The Edge) in Springfield.

Driven by the energy of the dance floor, Mario specializes in mixing music in key with smooth vocals and powerful basslines to create an unforgettable experience for his audience.
